# Khadok Store — Policies & Company Info

## Return Policy

Customers may return any unopened item within 21 days of the delivery date
for a full refund. Opened electronics can only be exchanged for store
credit, not a cash refund. Items marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned
under any circumstances.

## Shipping Policy

Standard shipping takes 4 to 6 business days and costs a flat fee of $6.
Express shipping takes 1 to 2 business days and costs $18. We do not
currently ship to PO boxes or outside the country of origin.

## Warranty Information

All electronics sold through Khadok Store come with a 1-year manufacturer
warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. The warranty does
not cover accidental damage, water damage, or unauthorized repairs. To
file a warranty claim, customers must provide their original order ID and
a description of the defect.

## Company History

Khadok Store was founded in 2024 as a small electronics and accessories
retailer. The company's mission is to make reliable tech accessories
affordable and easy to order online, with same-day processing on most
orders placed before 2 PM local time.

## Customer Support Hours

Customer support is available Sunday through Thursday, 9 AM to 6 PM
(local time). Support is closed on Fridays and Saturdays. The average
first-response time for support tickets is under 4 hours during business
hours.

## Loyalty Program

Customers who spend over $200 in a calendar year automatically become
"Gold Members," which grants free express shipping on all future orders
and early access to sales events. Gold Member status is reviewed and
reset at the start of each calendar year.
